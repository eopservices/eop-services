2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Identity Information: First name, last name, email address
  • Contact Information: Phone number
  • Profile Information: Profile picture (avatar), job title/position, date of birth (optional)
  • Authentication Credentials: Password (stored in hashed form, never in plain text)
  • Professional Information: Licenses and certifications (type, name, expiry date, issuer)

2.2 Email Integration Data (Optional)

If you choose to connect your email accounts, we collect:

  • OAuth Credentials: Microsoft or Google authentication tokens (encrypted at rest)
  • Email Content: Messages, threads, subject lines, sender/recipient information
  • Email Attachments: Files attached to emails
  • Email Metadata: Timestamps, read status, thread identifiers

2.3 Work and Project Data

Through your use of the App, we collect:

  • Project Information: Project names, notes, tags, associated clients
  • Task Information: Task descriptions, due dates, status, assignments
  • Booking Information: Dates, times, phases, completion status, notes
  • Client Information: Company names, ABN (Australian Business Number), contact details
  • Messages: Content of messages exchanged within the App
  • Attachments: Images, videos, and documents you upload

2.4 Device and Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Location Data: Geographic coordinates (used solely for weather display, not stored)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: Features used, timestamps of actions

2.5 Feedback and Support Data

When you contact us or submit feedback:

  • Bug Reports: Issue descriptions, screenshots
  • Improvement Suggestions: Feature requests and feedback

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure, which utilizes Amazon Web Services (AWS) data centers. Data may be stored in the United States or European Union regions.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at Rest: OAuth tokens and sensitive credentials encrypted in the database
  • Access Controls: Row-level security policies restricting data access
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing using industry-standard algorithms
  • Audit Logging: Comprehensive logs of data modifications

5.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

Data Type Retention Period
Account Information Until you delete your account
Email Integration Data Until you disconnect your email account + 30 days
Work Data (Projects, Tasks, etc.) Until you delete items + 30 days
Soft-Deleted Items 30 days before permanent deletion
Audit Logs Indefinitely for security and compliance
Attachments Until associated item is permanently deleted

Soft Delete Process

When you delete items in the App:

  1. Items are marked as "soft deleted" and hidden from view
  2. You can restore items within 30 days via Profile → Restore
  3. After 30 days, items are permanently and irreversibly deleted
  4. Associated files are queued for deletion from storage

7. Your Privacy Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

7.2 Additional Rights Under GDPR (EU/UK Residents)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom:

  • Right to Erasure: Request complete deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Automated Decision-Making: Right not to be subject to solely automated decisions
  • Supervisory Authority: Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Contract performance (providing the App services)

  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Categories of Information Collected: Identifiers, professional information, geolocation data, internet activity, inferences.

We Do Not Sell Personal Information. We have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months.
