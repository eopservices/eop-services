2. Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

Personal Information (also referred to as Personally Identifiable Information or PII)
Means any information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular individual or household. This includes, but is not limited to:
  • Identifiers such as name, email address, phone number, postal address, or online identifiers
  • Professional or employment-related information
  • Authentication credentials (passwords stored in hashed form)
  • Device and technical information linked to an individual
  • Any other information that, alone or in combination with other data, can identify an individual
Sensitive Information
Means personal information that reveals racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, genetic data, biometric data, health information, or information about sex life or sexual orientation. We do not intentionally collect sensitive information through the App.
Processing
Means any operation performed on personal information, whether by automated means, including collection, recording, organisation, structuring, storage, adaptation, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure, dissemination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.
Data Controller / Data Processor
Frontier acts as the Data Controller for personal information collected through the App. Third-party service providers act as Data Processors on our behalf.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure, which utilizes Amazon Web Services (AWS) data centers. Data may be stored in the United States or European Union regions.

6.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at Rest: OAuth tokens and sensitive credentials encrypted in the database
  • Access Controls: Row-level security policies restricting data access
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing using industry-standard algorithms
  • Audit Logging: Comprehensive logs of data modifications

6.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach. Under the Privacy Legislation Amendment (Enforcement and Other Measures) Act 2022, we are required to notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) and affected individuals of eligible data breaches.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

Data Type Retention Period

Email Integration Data Until you disconnect your email account + 30 days
Work Data (Projects, Tasks, etc.) Until you delete items + 30 days
Soft-Deleted Items 30 days before permanent deletion

Soft Delete Process

When you delete items in the App:

  • Items are marked as "soft deleted" and hidden from view
  • You can restore items within 30 days via Profile → Restore
  • After 30 days, items are permanently and irreversibly deleted
  • Associated files are queued for deletion from storage

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom:

  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing

  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Categories of Personal Information Collected: Identifiers, professional information, internet or network activity, inferences drawn from the above.

We Do Not Sell or Share Personal Information. We have not sold or shared personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising in the preceding 12 months.
